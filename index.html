FAQs

What is Frontend Mentor, and how will it help me?

Frontend Mentor is a platform designed to help you improve your web development skills, particularly in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It provides real-world coding challenges that mimic professional workflows.

Is Frontend Mentor free?

Frontend Mentor is a great platform for web developers to hone their skills. The good news is that you can access the majority of their challenges for free! This allows you to practice building websites with real-world designs. They do have some premium challenges that require a paid subscription, but the free tier offers a solid foundation for learning and building a portfolio.

Can i use Frontend Mentor projects in my portfolio?

Absolutely! Frontend Mentor actually encourages using their projects in your portfolio. That's one of their main goals - to help developers build a portfolio with professional-looking websites. Just be sure to clearly explain what the project was and what specifically you contributed to it.

How can i get help if I'm stuck on a challenge?

Frontend Mentor has a thriving Discord community specifically designed to help you with challenges. Join their Discord server and head to the "#help" channel. There, you can ask questions about specific challenges and get assistance from other developers!
